About Fraud Watch

Purpose And Technology Stack

Fraud Watch protects digital payments by identifying suspicious transactions early, supporting secure workflows, and improving fraud response for both administrators and users.

  • Purpose: Prevent transaction fraud, support compliance checks, and provide actionable forensic visibility.
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, and JavaScript for responsive, interactive user experience.
  • Backend: Python with Flask, SQLite storage, and integrated monitoring routes.
  • Intelligence Layer: ML-assisted fraud scoring with rule-based safeguards for dependable detection.
